Park Chicago - Meter Relocation Simulator

A tool for exploring how Chicago parking meters could be relocated away from bike and pedestrian infrastructure to higher-revenue commercial areas, subject to the CPM concession contract constraints.

Version 10
Drag-and-drop simulator
Manual relocation workflow. Select flagged terminals, propose destinations from pre-computed proposals, and apply moves. Includes permit zone utilization heatmap and business license demand scoring.
Stable
Version 11
Scenario simulator
Slider-based scenario engine. Set relocation scope, rate increases, and removal targets; apply the full scenario in one step. Before/After map view with summary panel and contract constraint gauges.
Active development
Data: City of Chicago Open Data Portal — Parking Meters, Business Licenses, Zoning Districts, Transportation Network, Parking Permit Zones
