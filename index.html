Park Chicago Meter Relocation Simulator

Example scenario and sandbox simulator exploring how Chicago parking meters could be relocated away from bike and pedestrian infrastructure to higher-revenue commercial areas without violating the Chicago Parking Meters concession contract.

Example Scenario
Proposal simulator
A pre-computed relocation scenario that prioritizes removal from bike routes, pedestrian street zones, and Better Streets for Buses corridors, and relocates those meters to high-revenue commercial corridors and side streets. Includes a before/after map view, contract constraint gauges, and a baseline-vs-proposal summary panel.
Sandbox Simulator
Scenario builder
Allows users to weight the same variables as the example scenario to explore effects and create their own version. Adjust relocation scope, rate increases, removal targets, job density weighting, and pedestrian street proximity. Supports individual meter group relocations by drag-and-drop or zone selection, and allows saving progress to resume later.
Data sources
City of Chicago Open Data Portal: Parking Meters, Business Licenses, Zoning Districts, Transportation Network, Bike Routes, Parking Permit Zones, CTA Rail Lines, CTA Bus Routes, Chicago Park District boundaries
Chicago City Clerk FOIA: residential parking permit counts by zone
Metra: commuter rail line shapefiles
Park Chicago (CPM): meter utilization rates and space counts
U.S. Census LODES WAC 2023: workplace area characteristics (job density by tract)
Cook County / City of Chicago: B/C/D commercial zoning parcel boundaries
CPM Concession Agreement (2008, as amended): contract constraints and rate cap formulas
